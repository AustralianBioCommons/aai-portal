Please register for the Bioplatforms Australia Data Portal by completing the form to the right.

Please provide your name, email, a username to use, a password, and the reason for your request.

All metadata describing the sequence data is available once you are registered, as are non-embargoed sequence files.

Once registered, you will have access to the publicly available portion of the datasets for the initiative you selected.

You can request membership of projects of interest to obtain access to restricted sequence files (projects currently under embargo).

Please contact help@bioplatforms.com with details of your username, the embargo section you would like access to and confirmation of eligibility (reason for access and link to the lead project team).

If the project you are interested in does not appear on this page, visit the Memberships page once logged in to request access.
